The Founders

Founded by Kermeen and Neville Bose. As a mother-son duo, they bring warmth and history to the table—Neville is the grand-nephew of the legendary Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose.

The Concept

A cozy 40-seater haven in the vibrant Art District. The name "Malido" references a rich Parsi pudding and translates to "finely crushed" in Pashto.
